About

Hi!

Welcome to my webpage. I'm Dheeraj, a graduate student pursuing MASc SYDE under Prof. John Zelek. I'm working in the Vision and Image Processing Lab ( VIP LAb) on the detection and tracking of moving parts in the automation industry and detecting faults in them.
